    Lucinda Mason

    About Lucinda

    Bio: Lucinda Mason was born in Northern Vermont. She received her BA in Studio Art and English Literature from Bennington College in 1998 and her MFA in Painting and Drawing from Concordia University in 2005. She has shown in New York, Montreal and Toronto as well as in numerous Vermont venues.

    Lucinda was an avid painter, traveler, art instructor and a writer for Art New England Magazine. She was affiliated with Canvas Gallery in Toronto and currently divides her time between Montreal and Vermont.

    Smooth scrollbar

    Some browsers smooth out the scrollbar when it is moved (like safari). This makes the Jarallax animations play more smoothly when the scroll bar is moved. Most browsers don't smooth out the scrollbar, this is visible when the down arrow is pressed. Enabling the smoothed scrollbar will animate the current progress of the Jarallax animation to the scrollbar position. This prevents animation jumping and animates the website even with small steps. This feature does require more updates which makes it more CPU heavy.
